Research Achievements

Mass Spectrometry Innovations

  • First known determination of fragment ions from field induced fragmentation at high pressures (660 torr) using drift tube-mass spectrometer
  • Designed and built a mass spectrometer interface for DHS funded non-contactless sampling system for explosive detection
  • On-site monitoring of Aflatoxin B1 in Pistachio nut using NMSU built technology coupled to Shimadzu 2010 mass spectrometer
  • Exploration of metabolites distribution in kidney and heart tissues using mass spectrometry imaging

Ion Mobility Spectrometry Breakthroughs

  • Designed and built a kinetic tandem drift tube for measuring decomposition rates of chloride adducts of PETN
  • Developed three-ion shutter-based ion mobility drift tube combined with quadrupole mass spectrometer for kinetic studies
  • Explored non-conventional drift tube designs for ion mobility spectrometry (NDA protected)
  • Evaluated performance of commercial trace explosives detectors (NDA protected)
